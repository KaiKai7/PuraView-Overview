Miami Dade Florida Sewage Outfall Deadline is January 1 2025

The plan is for the Miami-Dade Water & Sewer Authority to stop dumping treated sewage offshore. This is done using a pipes that spew the treated sewage into the ocean. This practice has been going on for decades up and down Florida coast. And according to the Miami Dade Water & Sewage Authority, up to 150,000,000 gallons a day of treated waste water is released. And what are they going to do with it then ?, Deep Well Injection. That's right, inject it deep into the earth, where it will miraculously disappear.
